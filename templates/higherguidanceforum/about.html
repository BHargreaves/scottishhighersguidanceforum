About

The Scottish Highers Guidance Forum is designed by a group of 2nd year Computing Science students at the University of Glasgow as part of our Web Application Development CourseWork.

It is aimed at those studying their highers in Scotland, in order to make it easier for students and teachers to post questions, upload files according to their specific subject.
